### We build software you actually own. 🪶

Saltfault is a tiny, independent software studio. No investors leaning on us, no ads to serve, no growth team quietly turning your attention into a line on a chart. Just two people building tools that answer to the people who use them — and that keep working even if we don't.

That's the whole pitch. Everything below is just us meaning it.

---

## What we believe

### 🔓 Built in the open, forkable

Every line is inspectable and yours to run yourself — free, self-hostable, forkable. If Saltfault vanishes tomorrow, nothing you depend on vanishes with it. No lock-in. No rug to pull. No "we've updated our terms."

### 🪶 Lean & independent

Two people. No VC, no ads, no surveillance, no exit strategy. We're built to *last*, not to flip. Small is a feature: it keeps us honest, keeps us close to the people we build for, and keeps the software light enough to respect your machine.

### 🔒 Privacy & ownership by design

Encryption and data-ownership are **architecture** here, not a policy page you're asked to trust. Your words live with you and the people you're actually talking to — not on someone else's computer, under someone else's rules, one acquisition away from becoming the product.

---

## 🐦 What we're building — Starling

**Starling** is Discord's shape — servers, channels, voice, video, DMs — without the company that owns every server. Messages go peer-to-peer across a network we call *the murmuration*; communities run on servers that belong to whoever runs them; everything is end-to-end encrypted. It's the beliefs above, compiled.
